IPF JES

Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis Job Exposure Study

Welcome to IPF JES.

A case-control study to investigate whether workplace environments are an under-recognized cause of idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) using an interview to collect information about previous jobs and a blood test to investigate genetic susceptibility.
